# Marketing E-commerce Grow — Marketing for E-commerce Landing Page

**App name:** `marketing-ecommerce-grow`
**Path:** `apps/marketing-ecommerce-grow`
**Type:** Service + Industry app
**Service scope:** Marketing
**Industry scope:** E-commerce

## Purpose

Landing page focused specifically on marketing services for e-commerce businesses. This is the most specialized level — combining Grow's marketing expertise with deep e-commerce industry knowledge to deliver highly targeted growth strategies for online retailers.

## Target Audience

E-commerce marketing managers, DTC brand founders focused on customer acquisition, heads of growth at online retailers, and Shopify/marketplace sellers looking to scale paid and organic channels.

## Services Highlighted

- **E-commerce SEO** — Product page optimization, category SEO, technical SEO for storefronts, schema markup for rich snippets, image SEO for visual search
- **Paid Shopping Campaigns** — Google Shopping / Performance Max, Meta catalog ads, TikTok Shop ads, Pinterest shopping, Amazon Sponsored Products
- **Email & SMS Marketing** — Abandoned cart recovery, post-purchase sequences, win-back campaigns, VIP segmentation, browse abandonment triggers
- **Social Commerce** — Shoppable posts, Instagram/TikTok storefronts, influencer seeding and affiliate programs, UGC campaigns
- **Content Marketing for E-commerce** — Product storytelling, buying guides, comparison content, video product reviews
- **Conversion Rate Optimization** — Product page A/B testing, checkout flow optimization, upsell/cross-sell testing, mobile conversion improvement
- **Retention & Loyalty Marketing** — Loyalty program design, referral campaigns, subscription model marketing, repeat purchase optimization

## Content Direction

- Lead with e-commerce marketing metrics: ROAS, CAC, email revenue %, conversion rate lifts
- Channel-specific case studies showing campaign performance
- Address pain points: rising CPMs, iOS privacy changes, attribution challenges, seasonality
- Tactical content — show that Grow understands the day-to-day of e-commerce marketing
- CTAs for marketing audits, channel-specific strategy sessions
- Seasonal marketing calendars (BFCM, Q4, back-to-school, etc.)

## Key Messaging

- "E-commerce marketing that converts"
- Every channel, every stage of the funnel, optimized for revenue
- We speak ROAS, LTV, and AOV — not just impressions
- From first click to repeat customer

# Marketing Grow — Marketing Services Landing Page

**App name:** `marketing-grow`
**Path:** `apps/marketing-grow`
**Type:** Service-specific app
**Service scope:** Marketing
**Industry scope:** All industries

## Purpose

Landing page dedicated to Grow's marketing services across all industries. This app positions Grow as a marketing-first growth partner capable of driving customer acquisition, brand awareness, and revenue through digital marketing channels.

## Target Audience

CMOs, marketing directors, heads of growth, and business owners looking for a marketing agency or partner that combines strategic thinking with hands-on execution.

## Services Highlighted

- **SEO & Organic Growth** — Technical SEO, content strategy, link building, local SEO
- **Paid Advertising (PPC)** — Google Ads, Meta Ads, programmatic, retargeting campaigns
- **Social Media Marketing** — Organic strategy, community management, influencer partnerships
- **Content Marketing** — Blog strategy, copywriting, video content, thought leadership
- **Email Marketing & Automation** — Campaign design, segmentation, lifecycle flows, CRM integration
- **Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)** — A/B testing, landing page optimization, funnel analysis
- **Analytics & Reporting** — Attribution modeling, dashboard setup, data-driven insights

## Content Direction

- Lead with marketing ROI and measurable outcomes
- Showcase channel expertise with real performance metrics
- Industry-agnostic case studies demonstrating marketing impact
- Clear CTAs to book a marketing audit or strategy session
- Navigation to industry-specific marketing pages (e.g. `marketing-ecommerce-grow`)

## Key Messaging

- Transparent reporting and measurable results

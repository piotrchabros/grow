# E-commerce Grow — E-commerce Industry Landing Page

**App name:** `ecommerce-grow`
**Path:** `apps/ecommerce-grow`
**Type:** Industry-specific app
**Service scope:** All services (Marketing, Consulting, Technology, AI)
**Industry scope:** E-commerce

## Purpose

Landing page dedicated to all of Grow's services tailored for the e-commerce industry. This app positions Grow as the go-to growth partner for online retailers, DTC brands, and marketplace sellers — covering every dimension of e-commerce growth from strategy to execution.

## Target Audience

E-commerce founders, heads of e-commerce at retail brands, DTC brand owners, marketplace sellers scaling to 7-8 figures, and e-commerce directors looking for a full-service partner to accelerate online revenue.

## Services Highlighted (All, E-commerce-Tailored)

### Marketing for E-commerce
- E-commerce SEO (product pages, category optimization, schema markup)
- Paid shopping campaigns (Google Shopping, Meta catalog ads, TikTok Shop)
- Email/SMS flows (abandoned cart, post-purchase, win-back, VIP segments)
- Social commerce and influencer marketing

### Consulting for E-commerce
- E-commerce business strategy and unit economics
- Marketplace expansion (Amazon, Shopify, multi-channel)
- Supply chain and fulfillment optimization
- Customer retention and loyalty program design

### Technology for E-commerce
- Storefront development (headless commerce, Shopify, custom platforms)
- ERP/CRM/OMS integrations
- Payment and checkout optimization
- Performance and scalability for high-traffic events (Black Friday, flash sales)

### AI for E-commerce
- Product recommendations and personalization engines
- Dynamic pricing and demand forecasting
- AI-powered search and visual discovery
- Chatbots for customer support and guided shopping

## Content Direction

- Lead with e-commerce-specific metrics: AOV, conversion rate, ROAS, LTV, CAC
- E-commerce case studies with real revenue and growth numbers
- Address common e-commerce pain points: cart abandonment, CAC rising, fulfillment complexity
- Show how all four services work together for compounding growth
- CTAs for e-commerce growth audits and strategy sessions
- Navigation to service-specific e-commerce pages for deeper dives

## Key Messaging

- "Everything your e-commerce brand needs to grow"
- One partner covering marketing, strategy, tech, and AI
- Built for the pace and complexity of online retail
- From launch to scale — every stage of e-commerce growth

## Development Notes

- **Skills:** Use `/content-design` for all UI copy and `/frontend-design` for all UI/UX implementation
- **Navigation:** Uses the shared megamenu from `libs/shared/megamenu` — highlights E-commerce as the active industry, links to all service-specific e-commerce pages
- **i18n:** All content must be available in both **Polish (pl)** and **English (en)**. No hardcoded strings — use the shared i18n setup for all user-facing text

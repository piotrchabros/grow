# Grow — Root Landing Page

**App name:** `grow`
**Path:** `apps/grow`
**Type:** Root / Mother app
**Service scope:** All services (Marketing, Consulting, Technology, AI)
**Industry scope:** All industries

## Purpose

This is the main Grow landing page — the top-level entry point that showcases everything the company offers. It positions Grow as a full-service partner for businesses looking to scale through marketing, consulting, technology, and AI.

## Target Audience

Business owners, founders, CTOs, CMOs, and decision-makers across all industries who are exploring growth partners and want an overview of available services before diving into a specific vertical.

## Content Direction

- Hero section establishing Grow as a multi-service growth partner
- High-level overview of each service line (Marketing, Consulting, Technology, AI)
- Industry verticals served (starting with E-commerce, expanding over time)
- Social proof / case studies spanning multiple services and industries
- Clear navigation paths to service-specific apps (`marketing-grow`, `tech-grow`, etc.) and industry-specific apps (`ecommerce-grow`, etc.)
- Call-to-action for general consultations / discovery calls

## Key Messaging

- "One partner for every dimension of growth"
- Full-stack services: strategy, execution, and technology
- Industry expertise with tailored solutions
- Data-driven, results-oriented approach
